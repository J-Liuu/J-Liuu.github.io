Game Projects

Cult Conspiracy

Duration: 2 Days (Game Jam)

Ghosts, cults, and magic—Cult Conspiracy has a little bit of everything! In this bullet-hell style game, you play as an investigative journalist infiltrating a mysterious cult while disguised as one of its members. This game was developed in under two days by a team of four during a university game jam.

Role: Created the main cutscene artwork and programmed the animated moving backgrounds for the game’s introduction title screen.

Heist Havoc

Duration: 12 Weeks

Steal valuable items and escape undetected! Heist Havoc is a stealth-based game where players must navigate a house, avoiding security cameras, guards, and even roombas. The game supports single-player, LAN, and online multiplayer modes.

Role: Designed and implemented the animated intro sequence. Developed the UI system that ensures item values dynamically face the camera for better readability.

Play Heist Havoc
